Workplace Incident Report

Use this form to report workplace violence, threats, safety concerns, harassment, injury, property damage, or other incidents that occur in the workplace.

If there is immediate danger or a medical emergency, call 911 first. This public form is for written reports only and does not accept file uploads.

When to Report

Report workplace violence, threats, unsafe behavior, injuries, harassment, theft, property damage, or other incidents that require management attention.

What to Include

Include the date, location, people involved, witnesses, and a clear description of what happened. Avoid submitting medical records or sensitive documents.

After Submission

A company representative will review the report and follow up when appropriate. For urgent safety issues, notify your manager immediately.
